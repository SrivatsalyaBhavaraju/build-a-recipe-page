My Favorite Recipe

It's simple, delicious, and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix the flour and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Add the butter and eggs, and stir until smooth.
  4. Pour the mixture into a baking dish and bake for 30 minutes.
